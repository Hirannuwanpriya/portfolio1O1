## Overview

WeRenewSneakers is a sneaker platform built for buying, selling, and restoring
sneakers — giving collectors and casual buyers a single destination to find
pre-owned pairs, list their own, or book a professional restoration service.
The client came with an established offline business and needed a digital
presence that could take online orders, scale with demand, and eventually
put the platform in customers' pockets. The project was delivered in two
connected phases: a validated e-commerce website followed by a React Native
mobile app, both powered by a single Laravel REST API.
