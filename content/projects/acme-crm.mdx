## Overview

Acme CRM is a multi-tenant customer relationship management platform built for
small and mid-sized B2B sales teams. The product replaces fragmented
spreadsheets and shared inboxes with a single workspace for pipelines, contacts,
and deal communication.

## Outcome

- Reduced lead-to-quote time by 42% across pilot tenants.
- Onboarded 30+ teams in the first quarter without engineering intervention.
- Sustained sub-200 ms p95 API response times on the shared cluster.

## Stack

Laravel 11 service tier behind a React + Tailwind front end, PostgreSQL with
row-level tenant isolation, Redis-backed queues for outbound email, and a
Resend integration for transactional delivery.
