## Overview

This trustee real estate management system was built to bring transparency and
accountability to property and fund management — giving property owners a clear,
auditable view of their funds, kept entirely separate from the funds managed by
the property manager. I joined the project as the dedicated front-end developer,
working from Figma designs and collaborating closely with a France-based
back-end developer over eight months to deliver a fully integrated Vue.js
application driven by a REST API. Communication ran through Slack for day-to-day
alignment and Google Meet for weekly syncs, keeping a two-person cross-timezone
team moving at pace without losing clarity on requirements or API contracts.
