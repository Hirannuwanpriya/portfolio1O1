## Overview

Brushwork ERP unifies inventory management, supplier purchasing, and an
in-store point-of-sale terminal for a multi-branch regional retailer. The
platform replaced a legacy Access database and three disconnected SaaS tools.

## Outcome

- Cut end-of-day reconciliation from 90 minutes to under 10 minutes per branch.
- Eliminated double data entry between purchasing and stock movements.
- Trained branch staff on the POS in a single afternoon thanks to a focused UI.

## Stack

Laravel monolith with Inertia + Vue 3 front end, MySQL with replicated read
slaves for reporting, Laravel Reverb for real-time stock sync, and a thermal
printer integration for receipts.
